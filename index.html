COVID - 19 Overview

Coronavirus disease (COVID-19) is an infectious disease caused by a newly discovered coronavirus.The COVID-19 virus spreads primarily through droplets of saliva or discharge from the nose when an infected person coughs or sneezes.

Read More

Symptoms

COVID-19 is a respiratory disease and most infected people will develop mild to moderate symptoms and recover without requiring special treatment. People who have underlying medical conditions and those over 60 years old have a higher risk of developing severe disease and death.

Read More

Prevention

To prevent infection and to slow transmission of COVID-19, do the following:
Wash your hands with soap and water, or clean them with alcohol-based hand rub.
Maintain at least 1 metre distance between you and people coughing or sneezing.
Avoid touching your face.
Cover your mouth and nose when coughing or sneezing.

About The Workshop

As the disastrous pandemic called COVID -19 has shuddered the whole world, still we are all trying to subdue its infernal culmination.
The doctors, scientists and other concerned sectors are making their best effort to help us out of this catastrophe with due support from the government.
What we, as public, can do is to cooperate with them and be aware of the on goings.

IIC VSSUT, Burla brings you an efficacious opportunity for that.
We are organizing an online "workshop on COVID-19", that too free of cost, to set forth the grassroot innovations to fight against coronavirus.
So utilize your quarantine time! Do register for our one hour online session!

Projects of Our Club

  • IIC VSSUT Burla organized an online hackathon called 'Hack For COVID-19' to provide an online platform for all the ingenious minds to come together in this fight against the novel coronavirus through their innovative solutions and build effective and feasible products.
  • We contrived DIY masks which are easy to make, very much affordable, serve all the purposes that the ones available in the market do and are designed according to WHO guidelines for COVID-19.
  • We created a chatbot to clarify all the doubts and prevent spreading of fake news about coronavirus, alongwith analyzing people's sentiments with a sentiment analyzer and further encouraging them to keep a positive mindset, do yoga, play games, etc.
  • We designed an app through which we can check if a person is affected by the coronavirus, notify the places where more than 5 people are gathering and track a person going out in shut-down areas.
  • We set forth the idea of online classes for completion of academic classes of the session.
